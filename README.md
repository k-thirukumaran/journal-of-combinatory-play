# Journal of Combinatory Play
https://journal-of-combinatory-play.k-thirukumaran.workers.dev/
A public notebook for exploring ideas at the intersection of generative AI, deep research, and creative recombination.
This space exists for a few simple reasons called out below.

## 🎨 Intent
Use generative AI tools as thinking partners, amplifiers, and provocateurs.
Scratch the curiosity itch through structured and unstructured deep dives.
Enable combinatory play—mixing concepts, disciplines, and methods in ways that would be impossible alone.
Work in the open, sharing what I learn as I learn it.
Publish simple, interactive web‑native notes that evolve over time.

## 📚 What You’ll Find Here
Explorations, experiments, and prototypes.
Notes that start messy and become clearer through iteration.
Essays, diagrams, and small tools built to understand bigger ideas.

>_"A living archive of curiosity."_

## 🌐 How This Repo Works
Each page is a standalone web‑friendly blog entry.
Everything is versioned, transparent, and open to reinterpretation.
The goal isn’t polish—it’s learning in public.

## 🤝 Contributions
This is primarily a personal learning space, but thoughtful issues, questions, and reflections are welcome.
